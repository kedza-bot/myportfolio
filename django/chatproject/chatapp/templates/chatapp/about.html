About Me

A brief introduction to who I am.

Welcome to my portfolio!

I’m Kelvin Ngwenya, a student specializing in Full Stack Development at the FNB App Academy and a self-taught web designer. I’m passionate about creating impactful websites and applications that combine functionality with great user experiences.
My skill set covers modern frontend technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with backend development using Django and MySQL. Whether I’m designing intuitive interfaces or building efficient backend systems, I focus on delivering solutions that are both user-friendly and high-performing.
I’m a fast learner who thrives in dynamic environments, enjoys solving complex problems, and works well in collaborative teams. My journey so far has strengthened not only my technical and analytical skills but also my leadership abilities. I’m always eager to learn, share knowledge, and contribute to projects that make a real impact.
